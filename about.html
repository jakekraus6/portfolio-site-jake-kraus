About Me

My story

Hi, I’m Jake Kraus from Southern California. I have a passion for all things creativity—I love photography, videography, oil painting, and illustration. I’m also a complete audiophile. Outside of my creative pursuits, I enjoy learning new things and spending quality time with my family. Between my career as an engineer and my passions outside of work, I found my calling in UI design. My engineering journey has taught me to master intricate processes and collaborate closely with diverse teams. These experiences have honed my ability to understand complex systems and translate technical insights into user-friendly designs.
